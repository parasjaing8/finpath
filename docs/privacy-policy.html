Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 11, 2026

FinPath ("we", "our", or "us") is a personal finance and FIRE planning app. This policy explains what data we collect, how it is used, and your rights with respect to that data.

1. Data We Collect

FinPath collects only the data you enter directly into the app:

  • Profile information – name, date of birth, monthly income, currency preference.
  • Financial data – assets, expenses, and retirement goal parameters you enter.
  • Authentication credentials – a PIN you set. The PIN is stored as a salted SHA-256 hash using Android's Secure Storage (never in plaintext).
  • Biometric preference – a flag indicating whether you have opted in to biometric login (fingerprint / face unlock). The biometric data itself is handled entirely by the Android operating system; FinPath never has access to raw biometric data.
  • Purchase status – whether you have purchased the FinPath Pro upgrade (CSV export). This flag is stored locally and verified via Google Play Billing.

2. How Data Is Stored

All personal and financial data is stored locally on your device in an encrypted SQLite database and Android Secure Storage. We do not operate any servers and we do not transmit your financial data to any external service.

3. Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect location data.
  • We do not collect contacts or call logs.
  • We do not place advertisements or share data with advertisers.
  • We do not collect analytics or tracking identifiers.
  • We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

FinPath integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Billing – used solely to process and verify the one-time FinPath Pro purchase (CSV export feature). Google's own privacy policy governs payment data. We receive only a purchase receipt confirmation.

5. Crash Reporting

FinPath may optionally integrate Sentry for crash reporting. If enabled, anonymised crash reports (stack traces, device model, OS version) may be sent to Sentry. No personally identifiable financial data is included in crash reports. At present, crash reporting is disabled by default.

6. Data Backup

Android's Auto Backup may back up app data to your Google account as controlled by the Android system. Sensitive credentials (PIN hash, biometric flag, Pro status) are excluded from backups per Android's backup rules configured in the app. Financial data in the local database may be included in your device backup, which is subject to your Google account's privacy settings.

7. Children's Privacy

FinPath is intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided data through the app, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Your Rights

Because all your data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it at all times. You can:

  • Delete any profile and all associated data directly within the app (Profile tab → Delete Profile).
  • Uninstall the app to permanently remove all locally stored data from your device.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will be revised accordingly. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at: support@finpath.app
